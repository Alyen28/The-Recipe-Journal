Cuscuz

A simple and really easy-to-make couscous recipe!

Ingredients

Original recipe yields 1 serving

  • 300g of pre cooked flaked corn flour
  • salt to taste
  • 1 cup of water
  • side dishes of your choice

Steps

  • Place the flaked corn flour and salt in a container.
  • Add water little by little and stir well.
  • After mixing, put everything in a couscous pan and bring to a boil.
  • When the couscous water starts to boil, put it on low heat and let it steam for 5 minutes.
  • Add your preferred filiings.
  • Serve and enjoy!
