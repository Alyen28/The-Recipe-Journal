Tapioca

Brazilian crepe-like pancakes made with tapioca flour!

Ingredients

Original recipe yields 20 servings

  • 500 g tapioca flour (bijou)
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 300 ml water
  • fillings of your choice

Steps

  • Place the tapioca starch in a bowl and gradually add the 300 ml of water.
  • Mix with your hands.
  • Sift the already wet tapioca starch, in small portions, that is, little by little.
  • Once sifted, heat a frying pan with Teflon.
  • Place the tapioca starch dough already sifted, enough to cover the pan, making a dough in the shape of a very thin circle, as if it were a pancake.
  • Leave it for a few minutes, until it releases from the pan, and turn it to the other side as if it were an omelette.
  • After a few minutes, it is ready to fill to your liking.
  • Serve and enjoy!
