Pavê

Delicious, creamy and easy to make chocolate pavê!

Ingredients

Original recipe yields 8 servings

  • 1 package of cornstarch biscuits
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 5 tablespoons of chocolate powder
  • 2 cans of condensed milk
  • 2 can of cow's milk (use the measure of the can of condensed milk)
  • 2 teaspoons of cornstarch
  • 4 egg yolks
  • 4 tablespoons sugar
  • 4 egg whites
  • 1 can whey-free sour cream

Steps

  • In a bowl, whisk together the milk and chocolate powder until it is completely dissolved.
  • Soak the biscuits in the milk and set aside.
  • In a saucepan, bring all the ingredients to medium heat and mix until you have a thick and creamy consistency.
  • Repeat the process done in the white cream.
  • Beat the egg whites with the sugar until you get a consistent cream, add the cream and mix gently.
  • In a large ovenproof dish, pour the white cream, half of the cookies, chocolate cream, cookies and egg whites.
  • Repeat the process until all the ovenproof is filled and refrigerate for 40 minutes.
  • Serve and enjoy!
